Our Mission

We believe in the power of mental health to transform lives and communities. Our mission is to: Equip young adults with the knowledge, skills, and support to develop mental resilience, foster emotional intelligence, and become confident advocates for their mental well-being through education, technology, community, and peer support.

What We Offer

Resources

Flip charts, notebooks, sticky notes, and pens—essential for hands-on activities and group exercises. T-shirts, flyers, banners, water bottles—to create a sense of belonging and promote the initiative. A dedicated room on campus that provides a quiet, comfortable environment for weekly sessions.

Peer Support

We have a volunteer graduate from the University of Rwanda willing to take us through some basic components of understanding mental health illness in addition to peer support training that we had asked for from the Solid Minds Counseling Clinic.

Community

Every Wednesday, we host a one-hour, thirty-minute in-person mental health peer support session on campus, where students can share experiences and support one another in a safe, friendly space.
